Our Story

A new chapter on Horsham Road.

For more than half a century, the Gallo's family has been feeding the Philadelphia area — first at our seafood house and Harv's Burger Bar in the Northeast, where neighbors have gathered since 1966.

The Scratch Kitchen is our next chapter: a warm, made-from-scratch neighborhood kitchen in the beloved space that Horsham has missed. Bread baked here. Sauces simmered here. Honest plates built from what's good and what's nearby — for a table where friends, family, and neighbors gather.
